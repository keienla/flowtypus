Flowtypus

Flowtypus is the perfect reproduction of the movements of a platypus in all it's grace.

Flowtypus is a small module usefull to make transitions between pages of a static website. The navigation between the pages of this website is maded with Flowtypus and you can try it at home! It's really simple to init, you just have to do something like: 

import flowtypus from '@keienla/flowtypus'
flowtypus()

And that's it! Simple right? Like this ths transitions will be automatic. If you want more infos about the possibilities, go to the infos page!
